Questions & Answers

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers — the way we like to do business.

Where are you located, and who do you serve?

We're based in New Bern, North Carolina, and proudly serve small businesses across Eastern NC — plus clients nationwide for our technology and AI work. Local when it helps, remote when it's smarter.

What services do you actually offer?

Technology — custom websites, software, business automation, secure online payments, and practical AI consulting — plus business & financial help: LLC and corporation formation, tax preparation and filing, credit education and dispute guidance, POS system setup, and buying gold, silver, and diamonds.

How does pricing and payment work?

The first conversation is always free. If we're a fit, most projects begin with 50% down and the balance due on completion — clear and simple, no surprises. We quote you honestly before any work starts.

Do you do taxes and credit help? Are you CPAs?

We provide tax preparation and filing, and for complex situations we bring in licensed professionals. We also offer credit education and help disputing genuinely inaccurate items — but we never promise to remove accurate information, and we'll always tell you what's realistic.

Can you help my small business use AI?

Yes — practically and honestly. We'll tell you the truth about whether AI even makes sense for your workflow before recommending anything. No hype, just tools that save you time and reach more customers.
