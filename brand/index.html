The Brand System

One identity — unified where it counts, distinct where each venture needs its own soul. Every site, graphic, and future build starts here.

01 · The DNA

The thread that runs through everything

No matter the venture, these stay the same. They're what make a Hahn site recognizably ours.

🅰️ One body font

Inter is the body typeface on every property, always. It's the anchor of the whole family.

🟡 One gold

#cba45a → #e0c07a — the signature gold accent and gradient. Same gold, family-wide. Never a second gold.

✝️ One faith line

"Soli Deo Gloria" in the footer of every property. It's who we are.

📞 One contact style

Text first, then Call — same number, plus email + "New Bern, NC." A sticky text-first CTA on every page.

▪️ Gold hairline + eyebrow

Section labels: uppercase, letter-spaced, gold. Under them, a short gold hairline rule. A recurring motif.

🤝 One promise

Honesty over hype — real claims, real reviews, no health claims, no inflated numbers. The brand IS the trust.

02 · Typography

Two voices, never a third

Inter carries the body everywhere. The display font tells you which world you're in — business or lifestyle/faith. Pick one bucket per site and stay in it.

Business voice — Sora + Inter
Built right. Priced fair. No sweat.
Confident, geometric, modern. The voice of the holding company and the trades. Used on: Hahn Tech Corp, Ready Tech Repairs, Gold & Silver, Ubuntu, No Sweat Electrical, AI & POS.
Headings: Sora 600–800  ·  Body: Inter 400–600
Lifestyle & faith voice — Fraunces + Inter
Patience is the rarest edge.
Warm, editorial, timeless. The voice of heritage, mission, and faith. Used on: Hahn Sparkling, Tortuguero Turtles, Flip the Cup, Methuselah, and the Founder & Story pages.
Headings: Fraunces 500–700 (optical sizing)  ·  Body: Inter 400–600
Retire these: Cormorant, Playfair (except Koala), and Georgia are being phased out so the family reads as one. Koala Tea keeps Playfair Display on purpose — it's a mature consumer brand with its own deployed identity. That's an intentional exception, not drift.

05 · Logos & assets

Use the real marks

  • Deploy the vector logos — hahn-tech-logo.svg and koala-tea-logo.svg (in Business/brand-logos/). They're crisp at any size and on-brand. They should be the actual header logos.
  • Never ship a Looka screenshot as a live logo — they include browser chrome. Download the transparent SVG/PNG from Looka first if you want one of those concepts.
  • Favicon: the circle logo PNG for business sites; a single emoji-SVG (⚡ 💧 🐢 🌊) for lifestyle sites — every site gets one.
  • OG share image: every site needs a 1200×630 og:image so it never shows a blank card when texted or shared.
06 · Copy-paste tokens

Start every new site from these

Drop the right block into a new site's :root and you're already on-brand.

/* ---- HAHN BUSINESS bucket (hub, repairs, gold, ubuntu, electrical, AI/POS) ---- */
:root{
  --gold:#cba45a; --gold-2:#e0c07a;
  --bg:#0a1120; --surface:#131f36; --line:#24344f;
  --ink:#eaf1fb; --muted:#9fb2cc;
  --accent:#5fa0e0;              /* swap per venture (see §04) */
  --display:"Sora",sans-serif; --body:"Inter",sans-serif;
}

/* ---- HAHN LIFESTYLE/FAITH bucket (sparkling, turtles, flipthecup, methuselah, founder/story) ---- */
:root{
  --gold:#cba45a; --gold-2:#e0c07a;
  --bg:#0b1a17; --surface:#122a26; --line:#254a44;
  --ink:#eef3f0; --muted:#9fb4ac;
  --accent:#7fb09f;             /* swap per venture (see §04) */
  --display:"Fraunces",Georgia,serif; --body:"Inter",sans-serif;
}
The rule of thumb: better every day, but never confusing or cluttered. One gold, one body font, one of two display fonts, one accent per venture, one clear call to action. When in doubt, subtract.
